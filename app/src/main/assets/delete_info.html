Delete Account

If you wish to proceed with deleting your account, please fill out the form below. Your request will be reviewed by Admin, and upon verification, your account and associated data will be permanently removed from our database. Please note that this process is manual and may take up to a week for changes to reflect.

If you have any queries or require assistance, we encourage you to visit our Help and Support Page before proceeding.

If you change your mind, you can always recreate your account by signing up again.
